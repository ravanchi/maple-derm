Dr. Nazli Ghiasi, MD, FRCPC, FAAD

Founder

Dr. Ghiasi is a Canadian & American board-certified Dermatologist and the founder of MapleDerm. She did her undergraduate and medical training at the University of Toronto and went on to complete five years of residency at the University of Toronto's Dermatology program. She served as the chief resident during her senior year of residency. She has the expertise and training to attend to medical and cosmetic Dermatological concerns of various complexity.

Dr. Ghiasi is an associate Dermatologist at Toronto Western Hospital and serves as a consultant with the University Health Network. She is involved in training of medical graduates and residents through her position as Adjunct Assistant Professor of Medicine with the University of Toronto.

Dr. Ilya Mukovozov, MD, PhD, FRCPC, FAAD

Dr. Ilya Mukovozov is a Canadian and USA dual board-certified dermatologist. He is dedicated to outstanding patient care, research excellence, and teaching. He completed his Honours Bachelor of Health Sciences degree at McMaster University, and his Master of Science degree followed by a combined MD/PhD degree at UofT. His dermatology residency was completed at the University of British Columbia.

Dr. Mukovozov works with leading experts in skin cancer surveillance and surgery, psoriasis, eczema, soft tissue augmentation, and neuromodulators. He is a fellow of the Royal College of Physicians of Canada, the American Academy of Dermatology, and the American Society of Dermatologic Surgery. His training and research experience enable him to work alongside his patients to help them achieve their medical and cosmetic goals.

Dr. Robert Bobotsis, MD, FRCPC

Dr. Rob Bobotsis is a board-certified dermatologist and Mohs surgeon in Canada and the United States. He is Assistant Professor at the University of Toronto with clinics at Sunnybrook Health Sciences Center focusing on procedural dermatology, where he teaches residents and medical students.

Dr. Bobotsis completed medical school at Western University's Schulich School of Medicine and Dentistry, graduating as the gold medal recipient. Subsequently, his dermatology residency and Mohs Micrographic Surgery and Dermatologic Oncology (MSDO) fellowship were pursued at the University of Toronto. This one-year fellowship was American College of Mohs Surgery (ACMS) accredited and completed at Women's College Hospital.

Dr. Bobotsis' main clinical interests are Mohs surgery and procedural dermatologic surgery, and his main research interest lies in improving medical education. During his residency, he completed a Master's degree in Systems Leadership Innovation (through the Institute of Health Policy, Management and Education), served as co-chief resident, published multiple articles in dermatology publications, hosted several learning opportunities for dermatology residents and received multiple awards for his teaching and research.

Dr. Kimya Hassani-Ardakani, MD, FRCPC, FAAD

Dr. Kimya Hassani-Ardakani is a double board-certified dermatologist in Canada and the USA, dedicated to providing exceptional care to both adult and pediatric patients. With a compassionate approach, she prioritizes understanding her patients' unique needs and concerns, ensuring tailored treatment plans that promote both health and confidence. Dr. Ardakani is passionate about advancing dermatological care and believes in empowering her patients through education and collaboration.

Dr. Ardakani earned her medical degree from McGill University. She then furthered her training at McGill University Health Centre, completing her residency in Dermatology, where she served as the chief resident and received the prestigious Resident Role Model Award. She then pursued a clinical fellowship in Pediatric Dermatology at Harvard University, where she was honoured with the Harvard Medical School Dermatology Fellow Teaching Award.

Beyond her professional commitments, Dr. Ardakani enjoys cooking, traveling, and painting. She cherishes time spent with her growing family.

Dr. Vijay Sandhu, MD, FRCPC

Dr. Vijay Sandhu is a board-certified dermatologist and fellow of the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada. Dr. Sandhu completed her Bachelor of Science with Honours at York University, where she graduated with the Faculty of Science Gold Medal, awarded for achieving the highest academic standing across all science disciplines. She subsequently earned her Doctor of Medicine (MD) from the University of Toronto, and remained there to complete her five-year residency training in Dermatology. During her residency, she was appointed co-Chief Resident, providing leadership among her peers and contributing to the academic and clinical advancement of the program.

Dr. Sandhu has authored numerous publications in peer-reviewed dermatology journals and has presented her research at both national and international conferences. Her clinical interests span the full spectrum of dermatology, including inflammatory skin diseases, skin cancer, minor surgical procedures, and cosmetic treatments.
